Who is this for?

Anyone doing academic research having questions about bioinformatics! Are you planning a new project? Are you stuck in some part of your analysis? Do you need help interpreting the results? We are happy to answer questions, small or large.

Who are we?

We are a local team of Bioinformatics scientists, part of the SciLifeLab National Bioinformatics Infrastructure Sweden (NBIS). NBIS supports Swedish life science research by providing bioinformatics support, infrastructure and training, in the form of local dropins, free and paid project support, PhD-student advisory program, national courses and more. Visit https://nbis.se for further information.

NBIS is a distributed national bioinformatics infrastructure, supporting life sciences in Sweden. We are located in Stockholm, Uppsala, Göteborg, Umeå, Linköping and Lund.
